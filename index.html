About Me

I am a dedicated Mechanical Engineer and researcher with a strong focus on bridging the gap between classical engineering and computational intelligence. Graduating from MIST with a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering, I have cultivated expertise in Advanced Manufacturing, Material Characterization, and Machine Learning.

My work spans from developing YOLO-based computer vision systems for industrial safety and medical imaging to conducting in-depth research on the densification of tropical wood species. I am passionate about solving complex engineering problems using data-driven approaches, including finite element analysis (FEA) and deep learning algorithms.
